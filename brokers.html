Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid commissions?

Commissions are automatically calculated and paid out based on the policies you write through GigEasy. You'll receive detailed commission statements and payments according to your carrier agreements.

Which carriers are supported?

We integrate with 15+ leading insurance carriers including Travelers, Hartford, Liberty Mutual, Progressive, Zurich, CNA, Chubb, and many more. New carriers are added regularly.

Is this only for contractors?

No! While we excel at contractor insurance, GigEasy serves all SMBs across industries including construction, manufacturing, transportation, hospitality, home services, warehousing, and more.

How quickly can I get started?

Most brokers are up and running within their first week. Our dedicated onboarding team provides personalized setup and training to ensure you're successful from day one.

Do I need to change how I work with carriers?

Not at all! GigEasy works with your existing carrier relationships. We simply streamline the quoting and binding process while maintaining your established partnerships and commission structures.
